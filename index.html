How It Works

From invitation to entry in seconds

01

Resident Pre-Registers

The homeowner logs into SubdiGate and registers their visitor — name, date, and purpose of visit.

→
02

QR Code Generated

A secure, time-bound QR code is created and shared with the visitor via link or screenshot.

→
03

Guard Scans at Gate

The guard scans the QR code, the system validates it instantly, and the visitor is logged automatically.

→
04

Homeowner Notified

The resident receives a real-time notification — their visitor has arrived and been cleared for entry.
